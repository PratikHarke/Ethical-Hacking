# 🕵️ Social Engineering

## 📌 Overview
Social Engineering is a technique used to manipulate people into revealing confidential information.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Understand human-based attacks
- Learn common techniques
- Explore prevention methods

---

## 🧠 What is Social Engineering?

Instead of hacking systems, attackers exploit **human behavior**.

---

## ⚠️ Impact
- Credential theft
- Data leaks
- Unauthorized access

---

## 🔬 Real-World Relevance
Many major breaches happen due to:
- phishing emails
- human error

---

## 🧾 Key Takeaways
- Humans are the weakest link
- Awareness is critical
