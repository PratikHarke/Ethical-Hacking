# 🧠 Penetration Testing Methodology

## 📌 Overview
Penetration testing follows a structured approach to identify and exploit vulnerabilities.

---

## 🔍 Phases of Pentesting

### 1. Reconnaissance
- Gather target information
- DNS lookup, subdomains, IPs

---

### 2. Scanning & Enumeration
- Identify open ports
- Discover services

---

### 3. Vulnerability Identification
- Detect weaknesses
- Use tools like Nessus

---

### 4. Exploitation
- Perform attacks (SQLi, XSS)

---

### 5. Post-Exploitation
- Maintain access
- Escalate privileges

---

### 6. Reporting
- Document findings
- Suggest fixes

---

## 🧠 Key Takeaways
- Security testing is systematic
- Each phase builds on previous
